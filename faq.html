What is KYC verification and why is it required?

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is a legal requirement to prevent fraud and money laundering. We need to verify your identity, address, and payment methods before processing withdrawals. This protects both you and the casino from fraudulent activities.

What documents do I need for verification?

You'll need: 1) Government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or provincial ID), 2) Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or government correspondence dated within 3 months), 3) Proof of payment method (bank statement or card photo showing first 6 and last 4 digits).

How long does verification take?

Document verification typically takes 24-72 hours once all required documents are submitted. You'll receive email confirmation when your account is fully verified. During peak periods, verification may take up to 5 business days.

Can I play before my account is verified?

Yes, you can play and make deposits before verification. However, withdrawals require a fully verified account. We recommend completing verification as soon as possible to avoid delays when you want to withdraw your winnings.
